细胞生物学笔记：内质网核糖体与蛋白质合成#

英文原版题目#

Biologists investigating the morphologic changes associated with reversible cellular injury perform a procedure on anesthetized mice to assess the effects of transient hepatic ischemia（肝脏缺血）. During the experiment, they clamp the hepatic artery and obtain liver biopsy samples at varying intervals. The samples are then examined by electron microscopy. Cells that are exposed to longer ischemic periods are found to have reduced numbers of ribosomes attached to the endoplasmic reticulum. This structural change is most likely to impair which of the following cellular functions?

  • A. ATP production
  • B. Drug detoxification
  • C. Synthesis of cell membrane proteins
  • D. Synthesis of cytosolic proteins
  • E. Synthesis of steroid hormones

中文解析#

知识点回顾#

  • 附着于内质网的核糖体（Rough ER - bound ribosomes）主要负责合成靶向细胞外分泌、细胞膜、溶酶体以及内质网自身的蛋白质。
  • 游离核糖体（Free ribosomes）主要合成细胞质溶质（cytosol）、线粒体、过氧化物酶体等部位的蛋白质。
  • 滑面内质网（Smooth ER）参与**药物解毒（Drug detoxification）、类固醇激素合成（Synthesis of steroid hormones）**等功能；ATP产生主要与线粒体相关。

选项分析#

  • 选项A（ATP production）：ATP主要由线粒体产生，与内质网上的核糖体无关，排除。
  • 选项B（Drug detoxification）：药物解毒是滑面内质网的功能，与附着核糖体无关，排除。
  • 选项C（Synthesis of cell membrane proteins）：细胞膜蛋白由附着于内质网的核糖体合成，因此内质网上核糖体减少会影响其合成，该选项正确。
  • 选项D（Synthesis of cytosolic proteins）：细胞质溶质蛋白由游离核糖体合成，与内质网上的核糖体无关，排除。
  • 选项E（Synthesis of steroid hormones）：类固醇激素合成是滑面内质网的功能，与附着核糖体无关，排除。

综上，答案为 C。
